ABOUT ME

Hi! My name is Camryn and I'm a self-taught artist from North Carolina. I'm a college student studying to be an art therapist. In my free time I love to spend time with my wonderful husband and my family! I also love all things art like watercolors, throwing pottery on the wheel, and just doodling.
